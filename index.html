About Us

Founded in 2020, TechVolunteers is more than just a student organization—we're a movement. We bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and meaningful community service, empowering students to create lasting positive impact.

Our diverse community of 200+ members includes students from Computer Science, Business, Communications, and beyond. Together, we tackle real-world challenges through innovative solutions and dedicated volunteer work.
